常见问题

如何升级到 Pro 版本？

打开 SnippetBox → 设置 → 点击"升级到 Pro"，即可通过 App 内购买完成一次性买断升级。Pro 版解锁无限代码片段、iCloud 同步和导入导出功能。

如何恢复已购买的 Pro？

打开 SnippetBox → 设置 → 点击"恢复购买"。请确保使用的是购买时的同一个 Apple ID。

iCloud 同步不工作怎么办？

请检查：① 设备已登录 iCloud 账户；② 系统设置 → iCloud → 确认 SnippetBox 的 iCloud 访问已开启；③ 设备有稳定的网络连接。

免费版有什么限制？

免费版可以保存最多 20 个代码片段。升级到 Pro 后可解锁无限片段、iCloud 同步和导入导出功能。

我的数据安全吗？

所有数据仅存储在您的设备本地和您个人的 iCloud 账户中。我们不收集、不存储、不传输任何用户数据。详情请阅读我们的隐私政策。

支持哪些编程语言的语法高亮？

SnippetBox 支持 Swift、Python、JavaScript、TypeScript、Java、C/C++、Go、Rust、HTML/CSS、SQL、Shell 等多种主流编程语言的语法高亮。

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upgrade to Pro?

Open SnippetBox → Settings → Tap "Upgrade to Pro" to make a one-time purchase. Pro unlocks unlimited snippets, iCloud sync, and import/export features.

How do I restore my Pro purchase?

Open SnippetBox → Settings → Tap "Restore Purchase". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

iCloud sync is not working?

Please check: ① Your device is signed into iCloud; ② Settings → iCloud → Verify SnippetBox's iCloud access is enabled; ③ Your device has a stable internet connection.

What are the free version limitations?

The free version allows up to 20 code snippets. Upgrading to Pro unlocks unlimited snippets, iCloud sync, and import/export features.

Is my data safe?

All data is stored only on your device and in your personal iCloud account. We do not collect, store, or transmit any user data. Read our Privacy Policy for details.

Which programming languages are supported?

SnippetBox supports syntax highlighting for Swift, Python, JavaScript, TypeScript, Java, C/C++, Go, Rust, HTML/CSS, SQL, Shell, and many more popular languages.
